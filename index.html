ABOUT DON
By Don DeDobbeleer
GALLERY

 


Don DeDobbeleer's love of woodworking and his inherent, as well as finely developed, skills both in design and joinery come together and stand out in these spectacular examples of custom art furniture.

His precision techniques ensure that each piece is of heirloom quality and will be treasured for generations.

 


Whether you're looking for something to complement your existing décor or have a concept, style or design of your own, Don will work with you to bring your dream to fruition. Don is also happy to create a one-of-a-kind piece, if you so desire. As you will see in the gallery, Don's accomplishments include a wide variety of styles including Contemporary, Federal and Sheraton, with an Asian flare or magnificent sunbursts.
