TESTIMONIALS

My son and I worked on this table. This is a good example of someone making me think outside my own design "box". He was very insistent in pursuing this particular style. This table has won a number of people's choice awards, and the lesson I took away from it is this: A client with innate design skills can lead an accomplished artist in new, unimagined directions that can lead to new innovations in your own work. We used Bubinga, Birdseye Maple, and Macassar Ebony.
