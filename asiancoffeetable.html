TESTIMONIALS

This coffee table is done in Bolivian Rosewood. It has a drawer that is nearly seamless in the front apron. The magazine rack is a woven shelf that is very stiff due to the Rosewood strips used in the construction. The flared legs take a substantial piece of lumber, as you can imagine. The drawer pull is a smaller version of the table with the lattice work in the center.
