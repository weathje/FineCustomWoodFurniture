TESTIMONIALS

This glass and Walnut coffee table featured a sunburst pattern that continued from the lower shelf, onto the top rim. The light star on the lower shelf is the natural sap wood in the Walnut. There is a 1/32" copper inlay around the inside of the rim. The glass top is installed from the bottom to eliminate the unsightly gap, usually found between the edge of the glass and inside of the rim.
